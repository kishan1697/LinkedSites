Preflight Checklist
  • ✔️ Check fuel levels
  • ✔️ Inspect control surfaces
  • ✔️ Verify navigation systems
  • ✔️ Confirm emergency equipment
Safety Procedures
  • ✔️ Review safety briefing
  • ✔️ Ensure seat belts are fastened
  • ✔️ Check oxygen masks
  • ✔️ Confirm exit procedures
Communication Checks
  • ✔️ Test radio functionality
  • ✔️ Confirm ATIS information
  • ✔️ Set transponder code
  • ✔️ Check intercom system
Final Preparations
  • ✔️ Review flight plan
  • ✔️ Confirm weight and balance
  • ✔️ Conduct final walk-around
  • ✔️ Ensure doors are secured
Emergency Equipment
  • ✔️ Check fire extinguishers
  • ✔️ Verify first aid kit contents
  • ✔️ Ensure life vests are accessible
Passenger Briefing
  • ✔️ Review cabin safety features
  • ✔️ Explain emergency exits
  • ✔️ Confirm passenger count
Weather Checks
  • ✔️ Review METAR/TAF reports
  • ✔️ Check NOTAMs for route
  • ✔️ Assess turbulence forecasts
Post-Flight Procedures
  • ✔️ Secure aircraft after landing
  • ✔️ Complete flight logbook entries
  • ✔️ Conduct debrief with crew
